Cleo Cwiek

Cleo Cwiek (born in 1994 in Warsaw, Poland) is a fashion model, published author, podcast host and a mental health advocate.

She is the founder & CEO of Mozna Zwariowac Foundation for Mental Health, founder of Poradnia Można Zwariować, a Warsaw-based psychotherapy clinic and an author of „Clou. How to be yourself when you're everything else” published in 2023 by Agora Publishing House.

Cleo is an avid climber, runner, technology and literature enthusiast. Her work blends media, technology and social impact.
